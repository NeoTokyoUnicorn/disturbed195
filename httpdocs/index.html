Lore

The disturbed is an innovative project where AI meets NFT. The disturbed is a unique collection of AI generated NFTs. NFTs have been generated by a GAN network using a Python script which works with Google Tensor.


The result is a collection of NFTs with utility: they can be staked to receive the governance token $AESYS or they can be used to partecipate to the Play 2 Earn game. The game is a visual novel narrating the story of Carrie, a young teen who has been diagnosed with ADHD (Sydrome of Attenction Deficit) and who will discover the deep spiritual implication of her "defect".

The current state of the project is in beta testing, so you can mint NFTs and use them but every activities will take place on the DevNet, without impact on real token. NFTs live on the Solana blockchain, currently only on DevNet, and game is offchain, excluded some interactions with the NFTs. Suggested wallet is Phantom.
